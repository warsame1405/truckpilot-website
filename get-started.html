ANDROID GUIDE ↓
📲 How to Install on Your Phone
1
Open Safari on your iPhone
TruckPilot must be opened in Safari for installation to work. Chrome and other browsers don't support this on iPhone.
2
Go to the app
In Safari's address bar, type:
app.truckpilot.ca
3
Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share icon (the square with an arrow pointing up).
4
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen". Name it TruckPilot and tap Add.
5
Done! Open from your home screen
TruckPilot will appear on your home screen like a regular app. Tap it anytime to open instantly.
1
Open Chrome on your Android phone
Google Chrome is recommended for installing TruckPilot on Android.
2
Go to the app
In Chrome's address bar, type:
app.truckpilot.ca
3
Tap the three dots menu
In the top right corner of Chrome, tap the three vertical dots to open the menu.
4
Tap "Add to Home screen"
Select "Add to Home screen" from the menu. You may also see an "Install app" popup appear automatically.
5
Done! Open from your home screen
TruckPilot is now installed. Tap the icon on your home screen anytime for instant access.

Everything You Need

One app.
Zero paperwork.

Everything runs automatically — from scanning dispatch sheets to generating your CRA tax report.

📦
AI Load Scanner

Take a photo of any dispatch sheet — printed or handwritten — and every field fills in automatically. TMW#, route, wait times, truck number. Done in seconds.

💰
Pay Tracking

See exactly what you earned per load including wait time pay. Every dollar tracked automatically. No spreadsheets, no manual calculations.

🧾
Expense & Tax

Scan receipts with AI and we auto-categorize everything by CRA tax line. Tax season is handled before it even starts.

Fleet Management

Assign loads, manage drivers, set custom pay rates per route and track payroll — all from one dashboard built for Canadian owner-operators.

Financial Reports

Income statements, CRA T2125, IFTA fuel reports and payroll summaries — all auto-generated and downloadable as professional PDFs.

Maintenance Tracker

Track truck maintenance, set inspection reminders and document everything in one place. Stay compliant and avoid costly breakdowns.
